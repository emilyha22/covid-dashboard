Wastewater Testing Results

UConn is pioneering a research method that allows us to regularly test waste water around campus to make an early determination on the presence of COVID-19. We are using two different measurements for COVID-19 in wastewater: E and N1 genes. We monitor 2 genes to increase the odds that we will be able to detect SARS-CoV2 even if there are strains that have mutated a mismatch of our probes. The graph below is a visual representation of the two different measurements we are using to test for COVID 19 in wastewater.
